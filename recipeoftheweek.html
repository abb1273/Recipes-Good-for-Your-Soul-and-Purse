Recipe of the Week

Chilli Chicken Ramen

Serves 2
Cooks in 20 minutes
Level Easy
Shopping
Ingredients
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp chilli paste
  • 1 pepper colour of your choice
  • 500g egg noodles
  • 1 tsp Chinese five spice
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • A splash of sesame oil
Method
  1. Add seasame oil, the Chinese five spice, chilli paste and ginger paste to a heavy based pan.
  2. Slice the chicken and fry in the sesame oil.
  3. Chop the garlic cloves, pepper and chilli, add this to the chicken
  4. While the vegetables and chicken brown, place the 2 eggs in boiling water for 7 minutes in a seperate pan, then drain.
  5. Cook the egg noodles as directed on the packaging, then drain
  6. Heat the chicken stock in a large pan along with 200ml of water and soy sauce, bring to the boil and then simmer for 10mins. While the stock simmers chop the spring onions.
  7. Once the stock is ready divide into two bowls, add the chicken, vegetables and chilli to your liking.
